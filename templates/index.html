Welcome to

PRAGATI

PRAGATI ENGINEERING COLLEGE focuses on imparting skills on cutting-edge technologies and shaping the students into disciplined young citizens of good character. The institution lays emphasis on practical experience so students can secure employment in industry and become entrepreneurs.

The courses are structured for linear growth and progressive insight into engineering subjects as well as soft skills. Since inception in 2001, Pragati has continued to build a vibrant academic culture.
